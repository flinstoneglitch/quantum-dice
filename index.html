⚛️ How your rolls are generated

Every day, a real IBM quantum computer places 8 qubits into superposition and measures them, producing a string of genuine quantum randomness.

That bitstring seeds a deterministic random number generator — so every roll you make today draws from the same verifiable quantum source. The IBM machine name and job ID are recorded, making every session's entropy provable.
